Server-Side CSRF Vulnerability Lab

This lab simulates a server-side CSRF vulnerability, demonstrating how attackers can make unauthorized requests on behalf of authenticated users.

Lab Instructions

In this lab, you'll simulate a CSRF attack where an attacker can perform actions on behalf of an authenticated user.
Follow the instructions below:

  • 1. Enter a new email below to update your profile.
  • 2. Click "Update Email" to update your email address.
  • 3. Open the "Attacker's Page" (below) in a new tab to simulate an attacker triggering the update.
  • 4. Observe that the email gets updated automatically, as the server doesn't validate CSRF tokens.
