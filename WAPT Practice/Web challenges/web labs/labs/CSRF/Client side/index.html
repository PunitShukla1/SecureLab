CSRF Vulnerability Lab

This lab demonstrates how Cross-Site Request Forgery (CSRF) works in web applications.

Lab Instructions

In this lab, you will learn how attackers can exploit a CSRF vulnerability to perform actions on behalf of an authenticated user.
Follow the steps below:

  • 1. Enter a new email address below to update your profile.
  • 2. Click on the "Update Email" button to perform the action.
  • 3. Open the "Attacker's Page" (below) in a new tab to simulate the attack.
  • 4. Observe that, even though you haven't clicked anything on the "Attacker's Page", the email gets changed automatically, demonstrating the CSRF attack.
